Career Timeline

  • 2017-2021 - Online instructor at AnimSchool.com for classes "Intro to Maya" and "Intro to Rigging".
  • 2015-2016 - Animation Technical Director at Blue Sky Studios, to evaluate and develop tools for animation workflow.
  • 2003-2013 - A decade of professional VFX experience as Lead Animator for Rhythm & Hues. I am proud to have been a part of such a wonderful and deserving group, winner of 2 VFX Academy Award films.
  • 2011-2013 - Helped form R&H's 'Animation Development Group' to assist the collaboration between the animation and other involved departments. Designed and developed tools, rigs, workflows and education in an effort improve artists' efficiency.
  • 2009-2013 - Acted as R&H Animation's 'Production Technology Liaison' (PTL), to represent the animation department in all studio-wide technology discussions, issues and decisions.
  • 2004-2013 - Developed and supported R&H's proprietary Animation Toolset, 'AnimTools'.
  • 2002 - Graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Academy of Art University
  • 2001 - Academy of Art University: Best Visual Effects for "Clone" Directed by Steven Salisbury
