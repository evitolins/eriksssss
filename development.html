AWS - Amazon Deadline Cloud & Nimble Studio

Working with our product, UX and management team, we were able to construct a customer-friendly onboarding experience allowing users to setup a Deadline render server in the cloud.

This involved designing, creating and maintaining a secure, scalable deployment of the service admin console and artist monitor applications. This includes integrating and maintaining a full CI/CD pipeline, metrics, alarms, dashboards, E2E tests and canaries.

Using both clearly defined guidelines set by AWS, and balancing with the needs of our target customers -- we worked closely with our UX team to polish and execute their Figma mocks into a fully function application.

Rhythm & Hues Studios

While a lead animator at Rhythm & Hues, I found time to write and support 20+ widely used tools for Voodoo, their amazing, proprietary animation software. Listed below are some of the favorites.
