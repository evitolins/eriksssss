AWS - Amazon Deadline Cloud & Nimble Studio

Web Development and CI/CD

Working with our product, UX and management team, our frontend team delivered a customer-friendly onboarding experience allowing users to setup a Deadline render server and monitor in the cloud. This involved designing, creating and maintaining a secure, scalable deployment of the service admin console and artist monitor applications. This includes integrating and maintaining a full CI/CD pipeline, metrics, alarms, dashboards, E2E tests and canaries.

Using both clearly defined guidelines set by AWS, and balancing with the needs of our target customers -- we worked closely with our Product and UX teams to design, execute and polish a final product guided by their Figma mocks and design documentation.

Blue Sky Studios

I had the pleasure of joining the Blue Sky team to develop and update several Maya-based workflow utilities for the animation department.

Artist Tool Development

While there, I was able to encourage more scalable and stable tool and UI design to promote:

  • Future-proof artist utilities that can support updated/changing rig designs from production to production
  • Streamlined UX which can allow faster, more expressive creative process
  • Improved UX by adding UI validation features and meaningful error messaging into UIs to help guide artists towards success

Listed below are some of the tool I built during my short stay.

Tween Easily set in-between keys for your selected controls. Realtime pose tweening slider option, with stable Undo stack.
Transposer Record, edit and reapply character animation in worldspace with ease.
SimpleSnap Record, edit and reapply picked objects' worldspace animation with ease.
ShapeFixer Add and manipulate animator-created blendshapes on rig-deformed characters
