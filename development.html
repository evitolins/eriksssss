AWS - Amazon Deadline Cloud & Nimble Studio

Frontend, Infrastructure, Testing and Monitoring Development

Collaborating with the product, UX, and management teams, our frontend team delivered a user-friendly onboarding experience that enables setup and monitoring of Deadline render servers in the cloud. This included designing, deploying, and maintaining a secure, scalable service admin console and artist-monitor application. We also built and supported a full CI/CD pipeline, metrics, alarms, dashboards, end-to-end tests, and canaries.

We balanced AWS design and security guidelines with customer needs, working closely with Product and UX to design, execute, and refine the final product—guided by Figma mocks and design documentation.

Blue Sky Studios

After evaluating the team's toolset and workflows, I proposed and executed scalable, stable tool and UI designs by delivering:

  • Future-proof artist utilities that adapt to production specific conventions
  • Responsive UI/UX that enables faster, more expressive creativity for the artist
  • Input validation and helpful error messaging to guide artists toward success
  • Clear user and technical documentation

Some of the most notable tools include:

Tween Set in-between keys via a real-time pose tween slider with undo support
Transposer Record, edit, and reapply character animation in world space
SimpleSnap Record, edit, and reapply world-space animation of selected objects
ShapeFixer Add and manipulate animator-created blendshapes on rig-deformed characters
