Bridging art and technology

I’ve always been drawn to the space where creativity meets technology. Over the years, I’ve had the chance to design, build, and launch everything from major AWS cloud services like Deadline Cloud and Amazon Nimble Studio, to proprietary tools and pipelines that shaped animation workflows at top-level VFX and animation studios. Along the way, I’ve also co-founded studios, developed games, taught animation and rigging, and built and launched widely-used utilities like RGB Notes and the AnimTools suite to make artists’ lives easier. Whether it’s cloud platforms, interactive experiences, or production tools, I’m passionate about solving complex problems in ways that empower both creators and teams.
