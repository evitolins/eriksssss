About me

I’ve always been drawn to the space where creativity meets technology. Over the years, I’ve had the chance to design, build, and launch everything from major AWS cloud services like Deadline Cloud and Amazon Nimble Studio, to tools and pipelines that shaped animation workflows at Rhythm & Hues. Along the way, I’ve co-founded studios, developed games, taught animation and rigging at AnimSchool, and built products like RGB Notes and the AnimTools suite to make artists’ lives easier. Whether it’s cloud platforms, interactive experiences, or production tools, I’m passionate about solving complex problems in ways that empower both creators and teams.
